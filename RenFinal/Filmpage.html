FLJ: Rise to the Rafters

In the first edition of "44 Films," a partnership between Syracuse Athletics and the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, Orange basketball legend Felisha Legette-Jack is profiled.

Director: Haley Fedor

This is our story

The Sports Media Center at Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, in conjunction with the Syracuse Department of Athletics, is launching 44 FILMS. The project is focused on short form non-fiction narrative filmmaking that puts the students at the forefront of every aspect of production, with the guidance of faculty and professional staff.
